What Employers Must Get Right — a 2.5-hour facilitated session taking HR leaders from Shadow AI anxiety to a human-first governance framework that actually sticks.

Session goal

Take HR leaders from governance anxiety to a clear, practical framework — with the hard questions asked along the way, not avoided. Leave with a commitment and a tool to act on Monday.

Facilitator's through-line

AI governance is not an IT project. It is a leadership decision about what kind of employer you want to be. HR is the function that must lead it.
